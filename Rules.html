RULES

RULES FOR ROUND 1

  • The participation for the event will be done in teams.
  • A representative from the team will be choosing the number.
  • The team size will be between 2 - 4 participants.
  • Everyone is supposed to submit their ideas in the google form/drive link given in the chat box and the Whatsapp group, named as TEAM_X.
  • All the participants are supposed to make the submission by 12:00 pm
  • 10 teams will be selected for round 2.
  • The qualified teams will be declared at 1:00 pm.
  • Everyone should join the meeting at sharp 1:00 pm.
  • The required presentation should be completed before starting the Round 2

RULES FOR ROUND 2

  • The team will be presenting their problem statement, and complete presentation.
  • Team will be called out in the order to present.
  • Participation of each member is compulsary during presentation.
  • The time limit of each team will be 15 mins.
  • Our judges will be declaring the results at the end.
  • Participants are required to use the template provided for presentation.
  • Participants are free to add any extra information they want to.
  • Participants are free to add images and graphics anywhere they need to. All the best!
