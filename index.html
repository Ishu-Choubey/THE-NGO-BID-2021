About

NGO - BID is 12-hour virtual ideathon conducted by FEPSI (Forum For Empowering People Through Social Innovation and Entertainment). This competition aims to  encourage our youth to dream big and live their dreams with the perfect wings. We believe an ideathon is like a marathon – it starts with a simple exciting innovative idea that could solve existing problems. That’s why we are asking you to share “Mera Idea Jo Badal De Duniya.” The competition will consist of two stages. In stage 1, the 7 most powerful of all proposed ideas, that have the potential to create a much significant impact through implementation, will be shortlisted. We will have a jury of industry experts and club members to carry out a fair and precise  selection. In stage 2, all shortlisted participants will be judged based on the prospects their idea holds and the progress in its execution. Participants are free to express their objectives through vivid presentations, conceptual models, working prototypes, products,  or working ventures. All we want is your best pitching!  Since, an ideathon involves the process of researching and brainstorming, to develop innovative solutions for common people’s problems, we expect you to keep up with the same spirit throughout the event and glorify creativity, and awareness.
