About

Ngo Bid is 12 hr virtual Ideathon conducted by Fepsi(Forum for empowering nation through social innovations)
The competition encourages our youth to dream big and gives their dreams wings. An Ideathon is like a marathon. It starts with a simple exciting innovative idea that could solve people’s problems. That’s why we are asking you to share “Mera idea jo badal de duniya.
In stage 1, we will short-list 7 most powerful ideas that can potentially have most impact . The selection made by our judges will be final.In stage 2, short-listed participants will be interviewed virtually to understand more about the idea and also to validate, if the participant has made any progress with the idea. By progress, we mean, if the participants has made any conceptual model, working prototype, product or working venture.
To put simply, in an Ideathon is a process of researching and brainstorming to develop innovative solutions for common people’s problems.
